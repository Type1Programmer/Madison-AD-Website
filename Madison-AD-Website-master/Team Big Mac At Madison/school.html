About the School

The place where dreams come true

Madison Academic High School is located in Jackson, TN at 179 Allen Avenue. We are a school that holds approximately five hundred students. The school is home to many prestigious clubs and teams, ranging from Academic Decathlon to the Forensics team. Every class that is taken at Madison is Honors(H) or higher. The teachers at this school never fail to embed our brains to perfection. Thanks to these wonderful teachers, we are a blue ribbon school! We have active students who support the school, and help make it a better place. We have over thirty clubs at our school that range from Creative Writing to Science Bowl. Our principal is the grand, Chad Guthrie, and our vice principal is the awesome, Spearman-Frankie-Taylor. Proceed to our school website for more detailed information!
