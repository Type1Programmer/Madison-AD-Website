Experiences

"Practice does not make perfect. Only perfect practice makes perfect." - Vince Lombardi

The Study Days

A fun lecture

Academic Decathlon is a place where bright minds compete for the recognition as the best, but it's also a time of memories. Memories that you cherish forever within your heart until the final beat. At Madison, we study everyday because we know we have to "take care of business," a quote alloted to our former principal, Ms. Janice Epperson. With those studies, we include debates about why quantum tunnling exist, or why the novel Transit is pretty "lit." Despite this, we keep focus on our one true goal: to win.

The Trip

A journey to competition

The journey to competition lasts the whole year, but a trip of deciding the victors is only a week. It's a drive of endurance and will. A will to keep studying even if the outcome is failure. This Madison Academic Decathlon team knows it. We "Mustang" or "Go Home." The trips aren't just filled with determination and studies, but also laughter. We feed off of it, the laughter, the joy, the feeling of happiness. This team isn't just about winning, it's about feeling you've gained something even if you haven't.
