Frequently Asked Questions

"The power to question is the basis of all human progress." - Indira Gandhi

What is Academic Decathlon?

Academic Decathlon is an organization/club that hosts academic competitions based on ten events that includes three objective events and seven subject tests. It involves high school students (grades 9-12) memorizing material to see who will learn the material the bests.

Who do you meet?

You meet people who will become your friends for the rest of your life. You make the best friends not just from your team, but also from other teams as well. Whether it's a team from China or the UK, the people you meet will always be a part of you.

Why should I do Academic Decathlon?

Academic Decathlon not only broadens your knowledge on diverse topics, but it increases your study skills. It helps students prepare for college work, and it gives those who aren't athletic or are just looking for a resume booster, a grand extracurricular to join.

What makes Madison's team so stellar?

Madison's Academic Decathlon team is all about determination and friendship. We stay focused on achieving our goals, but we enjoy the bonds we spark along the way.

How do I tryout for the team?

If you would like to tryout for our team, please visit this Contact page.

What if I'm not good in a event like math or essay?

That's fine because most people tend to have at least one weakness! Thankfully, there are actually ten events to do with a chance at one thousand points each for a combined and (unbelievable) ten thousand maximum score! The ten events are:

I. Literature

II. Music

III. Art

IV. Science

V. Mathematics

VI. Economics

VII. Social Science

VIII. Essay

IX. Speech

X. Interview

Is this the class for me?

Yes, this is the "family" that makes you "stang" proud.
