Friendships

"There are no strangers here. Only friends you haven't met yet." - William Butler Yeats

Lifelong Friends

Bonds that stay forever interlocked

Academic Decathlon isn't just about studying and achieving a gold medal. The people you meet in Academic Decathlon diversify your view on the world at large, and open up doors to eternal comrades. Academic Decathlon takes countless hours to study, organize, and participate in, along with the rigorous high school classes that we have to take. Throughout the year, Madison students, who at the beginning were mere strangers, become best friends. The hours that we put in together makes us a team that learns what the other prefers, whether it's a chocolate sundae or a bowl of rice. Our team chemistry comes from colloboration on and off the school grounds. Our team makes bonds not only with each member, but the other teams from different cities, states, and countries. We recently traveled to Alaska in late April through early May for the national competition in 2016. While we were there, we were introduced to a team from Shanghai, who invited us to dinner. We shared laughs, stories, cultural traditions, and photos with a team from China through an Academic Decathlon competition. This team isn't just academically stellar, but is also an amiable family of interesting stories.
